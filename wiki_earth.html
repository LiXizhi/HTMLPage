地球 Earth

地球（Earth）是太阳系八大行星之一，按离太阳由近及远的次序排第三。其质量约为5.972×10²⁴千克，是最大的岩石行星，也是密度最高的行星，另外它还有一个天然卫星—月球。至今为止，地球是已知唯一存在生命、表面存在液态水的行星。 Earth is one of the eight planets in the solar system, ranking third in distance from the Sun. With a mass of approximately 5.972×10²⁴ kilograms, it is the largest rocky planet and the densest planet, with a natural satellite - the Moon. To date, Earth is the only known planet with life and liquid water on its surface.

地球同时进行自转和公转运动，分别产生了昼夜及四季的变化更替，自转周期为一日，公转周期为一年，自转轨道面称为赤道面，公转轨道面称为黄道面，两者之间的夹角称为黄赤交角。 Earth simultaneously undergoes rotation and revolution, which produce the alternation of day and night and the four seasons respectively. The rotation period is one day, and the revolution period is one year. The rotation orbital plane is called the equatorial plane, and the revolution orbital plane is called the ecliptic plane. The angle between them is called the obliquity of the ecliptic.

形成与演化 Formation and Evolution

星周盘概念图 Conceptual diagram of a protoplanetary disk

地球形成 Earth Formation

根据放射性定年法的测量结果，太阳系大约在45.6±0.08亿年前形成，而原生地球大约形成于45.4±0.04亿年前。从理论上讲，太阳系中的天体与太阳是一起形成和演化的，太阳星云通过引力坍缩从分子云中划分出一定体积，分子云开始旋转并变化形成星周盘，之后行星与太阳一起从该盘中生长出来。 According to radiometric dating measurements, the solar system formed approximately 4.56±0.08 billion years ago, while the proto-Earth formed about 4.54±0.04 billion years ago. Theoretically, the celestial bodies in the solar system formed and evolved together with the Sun. The solar nebula separated a certain volume from the molecular cloud through gravitational collapse, and the molecular cloud began to rotate and change to form a protostellar disk, after which planets grew from the disk along with the Sun.

月球大约形成于45.3亿年前，但是关于月球起源的研究目前还没有定论，目前最受欢迎的是大碰撞说。该假说表示对月球是由一个火星大小、质量约为地球质量10%的天体忒伊亚（Theia）与地球相撞后，从地球释放出来的物质吸积而形成，还有一部分质量熔入了地球。 The Moon formed approximately 4.53 billion years ago, but research on the Moon's origin has not yet reached a definitive conclusion. Currently, the most popular theory is the Giant Impact Hypothesis. This hypothesis states that the Moon was formed by the accretion of material released from Earth after a Mars-sized celestial body called Theia, with a mass of about 10% of Earth's mass, collided with Earth, with some of the mass merging into Earth.

物理特性 Physical Properties

内部结构 Internal Structure

地球的结构可以通过流变学等机械特性、化学特性两种方式定义，从力学上来说，它可分为岩石圈、软流圈、中层地幔、外核和内核。从化学上来说，地球可分为地壳、上地幔、下地幔、外核和内核。 Earth's structure can be defined in two ways: mechanical properties such as rheology and chemical properties. Mechanically, it can be divided into the lithosphere, asthenosphere, middle mantle, outer core, and inner core. Chemically, Earth can be divided into the crust, upper mantle, lower mantle, outer core, and inner core.

板块构造 Plate Tectonics

板块构造 Plate Tectonics

板块构造是岩石圈的组成成分，是地球的地壳和上地幔的机械刚性外层，板块则是刚性部分，位于软流圈顶部，软流圈是上地幔的固体但粘性较小的部分，可以与板块一起流动和移动。板块运动的过程会产生地震、火山活动、造山和海沟等活动。 Plate tectonics is the composition of the lithosphere, the mechanically rigid outer layer of Earth's crust and upper mantle. The plates are the rigid parts located on top of the asthenosphere, which is the solid but less viscous part of the upper mantle that can flow and move with the plates. The process of plate movement produces earthquakes, volcanic activity, mountain building, and oceanic trenches.

大气层 Atmosphere

地球大气层是一层气体，统称为空气，由地球引力保留在地球周围，形成行星大气层。大气层的质量约为5.15×10¹⁸公斤，主要成分是氮、氧和氩，可以产生压力，吸收大部分流星体和紫外线太阳辐射，通过保温使地表变暖，使生命和液态水存在于地球表面，并降低昼夜之间的极端温度。 The Earth's atmosphere is a layer of gases, collectively called air, retained around Earth by Earth's gravity, forming the planetary atmosphere. The atmosphere has a mass of approximately 5.15×10¹⁸ kilograms, mainly composed of nitrogen, oxygen, and argon. It can generate pressure, absorb most meteoroids and ultraviolet solar radiation, warm the surface through insulation, enable life and liquid water to exist on Earth's surface, and reduce extreme temperature differences between day and night.

截至2023年，按摩尔分数计算，干燥空气含有78.08%氮气、20.95%氧气、0.93%氩气、0.04%二氧化碳和少量其他气体。空气中还含有不同数量的水蒸气，约占大气质量的0.25%。 As of 2023, by molar fraction, dry air contains 78.08% nitrogen, 20.95% oxygen, 0.93% argon, 0.04% carbon dioxide, and small amounts of other gases. Air also contains varying amounts of water vapor, accounting for about 0.25% of atmospheric mass.

地球上的生命 Life on Earth

截至2023年，地球是已知唯一适合生命居住的地方，地球形成后几亿年，生命就开始在地球早期的水体中发展。之后生命栖息在地球上许多特定的生态系统中，并最终在全球范围内扩展，形成一个总体生物圈。地球为生命提供液态水，提供维持新陈代谢的足够能量。 As of 2023, Earth is the only known place suitable for life. Several hundred million years after Earth's formation, life began to develop in Earth's early water bodies. Life then inhabited many specific ecosystems on Earth and eventually expanded globally, forming an overall biosphere. Earth provides liquid water for life and sufficient energy to maintain metabolism.

大约40亿年前，化学反应产生了第一个自我复制的分子。5亿年后，所有当前生命的最后一个共同祖先出现。光合作用使生命形式能够直接收获太阳的能量，因此产出大量的氧气，并由于与紫外线太阳辐射的相互作用，在高层大气中形成了保护性臭氧层。 About 4 billion years ago, chemical reactions produced the first self-replicating molecules. 500 million years later, the last common ancestor of all current life appeared. Photosynthesis enabled life forms to directly harvest energy from the Sun, thereby producing large amounts of oxygen and, due to interactions with ultraviolet solar radiation, forming a protective ozone layer in the upper atmosphere.

相关文化 Cultural Significance

地球在神话中被人类视为神，特别是女神。在许多文化中，母亲女神也是主要的生育神，许多宗教中的创世神话都涉及到自然神灵创造地球。在古希腊神话中，盖娅(Gaia)是地位最高的女神，她被称为地球母亲、自然之母，是最早的神，没有她就没有万物和生命。 Earth has been viewed by humans as a deity in mythology, particularly as a goddess. In many cultures, mother goddesses are also major fertility deities, and creation myths in many religions involve natural spirits creating the Earth. In ancient Greek mythology, Gaia was the highest-ranking goddess, called Earth Mother and Mother Nature, the earliest deity without whom there would be no creation or life.

世界地球日是每年的4月22日，该活动起源于美国，1970年4月22日，美国首次举行了声势浩大的"地球日"活动，是人类有史以来第一次规模宏大的群众性环境保护运动。1990年4月22日，全世界140多个国家、2亿多人同时在各地举行多种多样的环境保护宣传活动，并得到联合国的首肯，从此"地球日"成为"世界地球日"。 World Earth Day is April 22nd every year. This activity originated in the United States. On April 22, 1970, the United States held its first large-scale "Earth Day" activity, the first large-scale mass environmental protection movement in human history. On April 22, 1990, more than 140 countries worldwide and more than 200 million people simultaneously held various environmental protection publicity activities in various places, which received approval from the United Nations, and "Earth Day" became "World Earth Day."
