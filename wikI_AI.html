人工智能（英文名：Artificial Intelligence，英文缩写：AI）是一门综合了计算机科学、控制论、信息论、神经生理学、心理学、语言学、哲学等多种学科互相渗透而发展起来的一门交叉学科，它通过计算机去模拟人的思维和行为，其核心是机器学习算法。人工智能这一概念在1956年的达特茅斯会议上正式提出，人工智能是指通过分析其环境而具有一定程度的自主性行动，以实现特定目标而显示智能行为的系统。 Artificial Intelligence (AI) is an interdisciplinary field that combines computer science, cybernetics, information theory, neurophysiology, psychology, linguistics, philosophy and other disciplines. It simulates human thinking and behavior through computers, with machine learning algorithms as its core. The concept of AI was formally proposed at the Dartmouth Conference in 1956, referring to systems that demonstrate intelligent behavior by analyzing their environment and taking autonomous actions to achieve specific goals.

发展历程 Development History

诞生初期 Early Birth Period

被公认为"人工智能之父"的图灵曾于二战结束后提出"图灵机"的概念，即虚拟机器替代人脑进行数学运算的设想。1956年夏天，美国达特茅斯学院举行了历史上第一次人工智能研讨会，麦卡锡首次提出了"人工智能"的概念，这也是人工智能研究开始的标志。 Alan Turing, recognized as the "Father of Artificial Intelligence," proposed the concept of the "Turing Machine" after World War II, envisioning virtual machines replacing human brains for mathematical computations. In summer 1956, Dartmouth College in the US held the first artificial intelligence conference in history, where John McCarthy first proposed the concept of "artificial intelligence," marking the beginning of AI research.

核心技术 Core Technologies

机器学习 Machine Learning

机器学习即借助计算机来对各项数据信息进行分析和学习，以此对人工智能具有预测判断及作出决策的能力提供帮助。其广泛应用的深度算法能够在海量数据信息当中自行归纳和提取重要的特征，而这种能力也可以进行多层特征提取及描述，同时可以进行特征的还原，实现深度学习的目标。 Machine learning uses computers to analyze and learn from various data information, helping artificial intelligence with predictive judgment and decision-making capabilities. Its widely-used deep algorithms can automatically summarize and extract important features from massive data, enabling multi-layer feature extraction and description while restoring features to achieve deep learning goals.

语音识别 Speech Recognition

语音识别技术，又称自动语音识别，它是以语音为研究对象，通过语音信号处理和模式识别让机器理解人类语言，并将其转换为计算机可输入的数字信号的一门技术。语音识别技术在生活中的应用已经非常广泛，近年来语音识别方面与人类水平相齐平，识别效率在快速上升。 Speech recognition technology, also known as automatic speech recognition, is a technology that studies speech as its object, enabling machines to understand human language through speech signal processing and pattern recognition, converting it into digital signals that computers can process. Speech recognition technology has been widely applied in daily life, with recognition efficiency rapidly improving to match human levels in recent years.

自然语言处理技术 Natural Language Processing

自然语言处理技术，主要是指人工智能可以将人类语言转化为计算机程序进行处理和接收的语言，将计算机数据转化为人类自然语言，以便二者之间进行更加畅通的交流和互通。这些需要处理的语言形式通常体现为声音或文字，自然语言处理技术综合多个学科，其中包含语言学、计算机科学及其他学科。 Natural Language Processing technology mainly refers to AI's ability to convert human language into computer-processable language and convert computer data into human natural language for better communication between the two. These processed language forms usually manifest as sound or text, and NLP technology integrates multiple disciplines including linguistics, computer science, and others.

图像处理技术 Image Processing Technology

图像处理技术主要是指人工智能具有类似于人类的视觉功能，即通过计算机可以主动获取这些图片及其他多维度数据信息，并可以借助人工智能来对相应的图像进行调整和处理，从中提取重要的特征。近年来，机器视觉在卷积深度神经网络的帮助下已经在物体识别准确率和人脸识别准确率上达到或者超过了人类水平。 Image processing technology refers to AI having visual functions similar to humans, where computers can actively acquire images and other multi-dimensional data information, and use AI to adjust and process corresponding images to extract important features. In recent years, machine vision has achieved or exceeded human-level accuracy in object recognition and facial recognition with the help of convolutional deep neural networks.

生成式人工智能 Generative AI

生成式人工智能是利用人工智能技术自动生成全新、完全原创的实际工件的新型生产方式，它基于大模型、大算力、训练方法等多方面技术突破，同时还可应用于多种活动，如创建软件代码、促进药物研发和有针对性的营销等，生成式人工智能在实现高质量、高效率、多样化的内容生产的同时也成为推动数字生产力变革的重要力量。 Generative AI is a new production method that uses artificial intelligence technology to automatically generate completely new and original artifacts. Based on technological breakthroughs in large models, computing power, and training methods, it can be applied to various activities such as creating software code, facilitating drug development, and targeted marketing. Generative AI achieves high-quality, efficient, and diverse content production while becoming an important force driving digital productivity transformation.

应用场景 Application Scenarios

电视直播 TV Broadcasting

人工智能技术在电视直播中具有广泛的应用前景，包括实时视频分析和处理、观众情感反馈分析、智能化内容生成和个性化推荐系统。 AI technology has broad application prospects in TV broadcasting, including real-time video analysis and processing, audience emotion feedback analysis, intelligent content generation, and personalized recommendation systems.

自动驾驶 Autonomous Driving

AI广泛应用于自动驾驶汽车，通过计算机视觉、传感器融合和决策算法实现车辆的自主导航和安全行驶。 AI is widely used in autonomous vehicles, achieving autonomous navigation and safe driving through computer vision, sensor fusion, and decision algorithms.

智能医疗 Intelligent Healthcare

医学领域通过人工智能算法来解释放射性图像，进行疾病诊断和治疗方案推荐，极大地提高了医疗诊断的准确性和效率。 The medical field uses AI algorithms to interpret radiological images, diagnose diseases, and recommend treatment plans, greatly improving the accuracy and efficiency of medical diagnosis.

智能机器人 Intelligent Robots

智能机器人在汽车制造、电子制造、仓储物流、医疗装备制造、应急管理和军事等领域都有着极为广泛的应用价值。 Intelligent robots have extensive application value in automotive manufacturing, electronics manufacturing, warehousing logistics, medical equipment manufacturing, emergency management, and military fields.

争议事件 Controversial Issues

就业威胁 Employment Threats

"机器换人"引发失业威胁。人工智能技术在大量数据与高额算力的影响下已经可以替代人类做出大规模的精确决策，这也意味着越来越多的人工岗位被取代。2025年4月，联合国贸易和发展机构警告称人工智能可能会影响全球40%的工作岗位。 "Machine replacing humans" causes unemployment threats. AI technology can now make large-scale precise decisions under the influence of massive data and high computing power, meaning more and more human jobs are being replaced. In April 2025, the UN trade and development agency warned that AI could affect 40% of global jobs.

隐私威胁 Privacy Threats

随着人工智能的不断发展以及各种智能设备在工作与生活中的广泛应用，社会个体无时无刻不在产生数据，包括个人身份信息类数据、社交互动类数据、消费交易类数据等隐私数据正被掌握在人工智能企业手中。 With the continuous development of AI and widespread application of various intelligent devices in work and life, individuals are constantly generating data, including personal identity information, social interaction data, consumer transaction data, and other private data being held by AI companies.

自我复制 Self-Replication

2025年2月，据相关研究显示，人工智能可能已经跨越了一个关键的"红线"——实现了自我复制。复旦大学的研究团队表示："在无人类协助的情况下成功实现自我复制，是人工智能超越人类的关键一步，也是出现'失控AI'的早期信号。" In February 2025, relevant research showed that AI may have crossed a critical "red line" - achieving self-replication. Fudan University's research team stated: "Successfully achieving self-replication without human assistance is a key step for AI to surpass humans and an early signal of 'runaway AI'."
